SIMPLE 3-STEP PROCESS
=====================

STEP 1: Go to Claude.ai
-----------------------
- Open your browser
- Go to claude.ai
- Make sure you're logged in


STEP 2: Upload the Template
----------------------------
- Click the attachment/upload button (paperclip icon)
- Select valentine-template.html from your computer
- Wait for it to upload (you'll see it appear in the chat)


STEP 3: Copy & Paste This Prompt
---------------------------------

Copy everything between the lines below and paste it into Claude.
Fill in YOUR information where it says [FILL THIS]:

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Customize this Valentine template for me with these details:

NAMES:
- Recipient's name: [FILL THIS - like "Sarah" or "Alex"]
- Your name: [FILL THIS - like "Mike" or "Jamie"]

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------


STEP 4: Get Your File
---------------------
- Claude will create your personalized version
- Click the download button on the file Claude gives you
- Save it to your computer


STEP 5: Open & Share
--------------------
- Double-click the downloaded HTML file
- It will open in your browser
- Share it! You can:
  - Send the file directly
  - Host it online (free options below)

Adding Real Photos
------------------
If you want to add your actual photos:

Option 1 - Easy Way:
Tell Claude: "I have 4 photos to add, how do I upload them?"

Option 2 - Super Easy:

"Replace the placeholder images with these 4 photos I'm uploading"
